================================================================================
FORMULA REFERENCE
================================================================================

ORIGINAL (additive weighted sum):
  kra_subtotal    = Σ (criterion_weight/100 × rating)   [range: 0–4]
  behavior_avg    = avg of all behavior ratings          [range: 1–4]
  total_score     = (kra_subtotal × 0.80) + (behavior_avg × 0.20)

  Example: kra_subtotal=3.0, behavior_avg=3.5
    total = (3.0 × 0.80) + (3.5 × 0.20) = 2.40 + 0.70 = 3.10

NEW (multiplicative — weight × rating × average):
  total_score = (kra_subtotal × behavior_average) / 4.0

  Example: kra_subtotal=3.0, behavior_avg=3.5
    total = (3.0 × 3.5) / 4.0 = 10.5 / 4.0 = 2.625

PERFORMANCE LEVELS (unchanged either way):
  3.60 – 4.00  → Outstanding
  2.60 – 3.59  → Exceeds Expectations
  2.00 – 2.59  → Meets Expectations
  1.00 – 1.99  → Needs Improvement
